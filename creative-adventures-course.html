Creative Adventures Course

Overview of this course

Learning various types of art allows children to express themselves creatively, enhances their imagination as well as helps to reduce their stress and anxiety. Creative Adventures is a course that has been prepared to let children embark on a creative journey and bring out the hidden artist in them. The course includes a variety of arts, crafts, and painting programs that are conducted to promote cognitive skills in your child.

Course Structure

Our course structure includes a variety of topics that are taught to children in this journey.

The main course structure consists of:

• Basics of arts and crafts

• Different styles of painting

• Different artists and what inspired them to explore their talent

Our course structure is formulated in a way so that children can learn various styles of art.
This helps them choose what style they want to continue learning in the future.

The various art styles taught in our course include:

• Persian Art    • Zen tangle    • Egyptian Art

• Still Life    • Mandala Art
