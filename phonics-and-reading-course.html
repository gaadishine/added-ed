Here's a sneak-peak of our

Phonics & Reading Course

Our course begins with teaching the child letter sounds, moving onto blending, teaching digraphs, reading target words, and gradually sentence reading and formation all the while the learning is consolidated with writing and experiential activities. After every class, 3 customized individual worksheets are provided to the child.

Overview of this course

We offer customized Phonics & Reading classes for each child depending on their unique needs. We aim for children to become successful readers and writers in their early years and beyond. Our focus is to enhance a child’s ability to sound out words, identify the letters and their corresponding sounds, and improve pronunciation.
