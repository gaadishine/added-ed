Overview

A program developed by state-level champions and international chess arbitrators where the child not only learns Chess but also enhances their IQ (Intelligence Quotient), logical reasoning, critical thinking, focus and concentration. Our classes are conducted by professional chess players.


Aim

Our Chess program is aimed at


  • Enhancing a child's learning, thinking and analytical power, and their decision-making ability.
  • Improve a child’s Problem-Solving Skills, Logical Reasoning, Brain Power
  • Enhance Intelligence to help the children strategize their lives and make thoughtful decisions from a young age.
  • Give a platform to your child to compete with National and International Students through our program.
